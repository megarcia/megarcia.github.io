WxCD

Weather and Climate Derivatives
  mapped from daily surface observations


Author: Matthew Garcia
PI: Prof. Philip Townsend
See: Garcia and Townsend (2016, accepted)

Introduction

WxCD is a collection pf python scripts for mapping weather (Wx) observations from surface meteorological stations and calculating climate derivatives (CD) over various time periods.
