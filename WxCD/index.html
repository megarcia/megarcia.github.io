WxCD

Weather and Climate Derivatives
   mapped from daily surface observations

Author: Matthew Garcia
PI: Prof. A. Philip Townsend
Ref: Garcia and Townsend (2016, accepted)

Introduction

WxCD is a collection pf python scripts and workflows for mapping weather (Wx) observations from surface meteorological stations and then calculating climatological and related statistical derivatives over various time periods. This project is part of ongoing work in the author's Ph.D. dissertation program.
